Our Thesis

A disciplined approach to acquiring Colombia's finest operators

Latin America represents one of the most compelling untapped markets for the search fund model. Colombia's growing middle class, improving institutional environment, and fragmented SME landscape create a unique window of opportunity for experienced operators.

We focus on profitable, established businesses with defensible competitive positions — where skilled ownership can unlock meaningful value creation over a 5–7 year horizon.

01
US PE is overcrowded & returns are shrinking
Too much capital chasing a limited number of high-quality businesses. Entry multiples have reached historic highs, and the best companies have already been PE-owned and optimized. US PE returns have declined to 5.8% (2022–2025) versus 11.6% for the S&P 500.
02
Colombian SMEs remain largely untapped
Large opportunities remain to professionalize operations across highly fragmented industries. LATAM companies trade at 3–6x EBITDA versus 7–12x in the US — a significant discount creating clear upside through operational improvement and consolidation.
03
Search Funds are the ideal model for Colombia
Colombia is too fragmented for traditional PE to enter efficiently. The LATAM search fund model is already proven: 54 completed LATAM acquisitions to date, with IESE-tracked funds demonstrating compelling returns. Search funds are uniquely positioned to identify and operate premier SMEs where traditional PE cannot compete.
